  • After you've collected 8 coins or reached 80 points you will automatically move on to the next assessment and 8 new coins will be loaded in. There are 7 assessments in total, with a Pretest at the beginning. Demo Day is the end goal.

  • The score is calculated as follows:
    1. 80 points = Assessment 1
    2. 160 points = Assessment 2
    3. 240 points = Assessment 3
    And so on. All the way up to 640 points where your completion will be 100% and you will have reached Demo Day.
  • If you run into a technology, it is an automatic game over and you will have to select a new character and start from the Pre-Test once again.
  • The player is controlled with the main arrows keys. To jump is UP, to move left is LEFT and to move right is RIGHT.
  • Your score and completion percentage are displayed to the left in the LMS bar.
